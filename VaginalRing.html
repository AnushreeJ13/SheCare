Birth Control Vaginal Ring

What is the birth control ring?

The birth control ring (AKA the vaginal ring, or the ring) is a safe and convenient birth control method that works really well if you always use it correctly. You wear a small, flexible ring inside your vagina, and it prevents pregnancy 24/7 by releasing hormones into your body. The ring has lots of other health benefits, too. There are 2 kinds of birth control rings: NuvaRing and Annovera.

How effective is the birth control ring?

If you use it perfectly, the ring is 99% effective. But people aren’t perfect and it can be easy to make a mistake — so in reality the ring is about 93% effective. That means about 7 out of 100 ring users get pregnant each year.

The better you are about putting the ring in your vagina on schedule, the better it will work. But there’s a very small chance that you could still get pregnant, even if you always use the ring correctly.



Who can’t use birth control rings?

Like all medicines, the ring isn’t for everyone.

Smoking and birth control rings don’t always mix

1.If you smoke 15 cigarettes a day or more, don’t use NuvaRing, Annovera, or any other kind of birth control that has the hormone estrogen.

2.If you smoke fewer than 15 cigarettes a day or vape nicotine at all, talk with your nurse or doctor about whether the birth control ring is safe for you.

Smoking while taking birth control with estrogen (like combination pills, the patch, or the ring), makes you more likely to have problems like a stroke or heart attack.

If you’re a regular smoker, you can safely use any other method without estrogen — like progestin-only pills, the birth control shot, the birth control implant, and IUDs.

Also avoid using the ring if you’ve had any of these health problems:

1. blood clots, an inherited blood-clotting disorder, or vein inflammation

2.breast cancer

3.heart attack, stroke, angina, or other serious heart problems

4.migraine headaches with aura (seeing flashing, zigzag lines)

5. uncontrolled high blood pressure

6.very bad diabetes or liver disease

Talk with your doctor or nurse about your health history. They can help you decide if the ring is right for you.

NuvaRing and Annovera rings are latex-free, so they’re safe for people with latex allergies.

What are the benefits of the birth control ring?

Birth control rings are a safe, simple, and convenient way to prevent pregnancy. The ring also has other benefits like reducing acne, making your periods lighter and more regular, and easing menstrual cramps.

1.The ring is an effective way to prevent pregnancy.

2.The ring is convenient.

3.The ring can make your periods better.

4.he ring can help prevent or lessen:

a. acne

b. bone thinning

c. cysts in your breasts and ovaries

d. ectopic pregnancy

e. endometrial and ovarian cancers

f. serious infections in your ovaries, fallopian tubes, and uterus

g. iron deficiency (anemia)

f. PMS (premenstrual syndrome)

What are the disadvantages of the birth control ring?

Remembering to put a new ring in on time might be hard. And some people get side effects that bother them (but they usually go away in a few months). 1.You have to stay on schedule. 2.There can be negative side effects. 3.There can be some rare but serious risks.
