How Do I Ask A Partner To Use A Condom?

Asking a partner to use a condom is one of those things which sounds incredibly simple but can be incredibly daunting when the time itself arises. Regardless of genitalia at play, condoms (if used correctly and consistently) are the best way to limit the spread of STDs and protect yourself against an infection.

What to Know about Emergency Contraception

Emergency contraception is taken when there has been a failed birth control method or when there isn’t one used. There are two types of emergency contraception, and this blog will educate the readers on the morning-after pill, how it’s taken, when to take it, its effects and side effects. Even though it’s an over-the-counter pill, it is better to consult a doctor on which method will be most effective for the individual.
